Program Notes & Guidelines

Important information for success

Rest Periods:

  • Weeks 1-2: 60-90 seconds between supersets
  • Weeks 3-4: 90 seconds between supersets
  • Weeks 5-6: 90-120 seconds between supersets (you're lifting heavier)

Progressive Overload:

  • Write down your weights each session in the Progress Tracker
  • If you hit the top end of the rep range easily, increase weight by 5 lbs next session
  • Example: If you do 12 reps easily, go up in weight the following week
  • It's better to use slightly lighter weight with perfect form than heavy weight with poor form

Form Guidelines:

  • Always prioritize proper form over weight
  • Control the eccentric (lowering) phase for 2-3 seconds
  • Squeeze and contract at the top of each movement
  • If form breaks down, reduce the weight immediately
  • Click the exercise names to watch form videos!

Cardio Intensity Zones:

  • Moderate: Can hold a conversation, 65-75% max heart rate
  • Moderate-High: Breathing hard, short sentences, 75-85% max heart rate
  • High: Can barely talk, 85%+ max heart rate

Recovery Tips:

  • Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night
  • Stay hydrated: ~115 oz of water daily (bodyweight √∑ 2 = oz)
  • Stretch or foam roll on rest days
  • Listen to your body - if you need extra rest, take it
  • Wednesday and Sunday are full rest days - use them!

Equipment Substitutions:

  • If dumbbells aren't available for an exercise, use the machine equivalent at Lifetime
  • Chest pull machine can be substituted with lat pulldown
  • Most leg exercises can be done with bodyweight if needed
  • Saturday classes are flexible - choose what you enjoy!

Success Tips:

  • Work out in the morning when possible for consistency
  • Prepare your gym bag and clothes the night before
  • Track every workout - what gets measured gets improved
  • Take progress photos every 2 weeks (same lighting, same pose)
  • Stay consistent - 6 weeks goes fast!

‚ö†Ô∏è When to Modify:

  • If you feel pain (not muscle soreness), stop and assess
  • If you're extremely sore, reduce volume or take extra rest
  • If life gets busy, doing 3-4 days is better than 0 days
  • Quality over quantity - never sacrifice form for reps
